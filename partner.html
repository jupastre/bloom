Partner With Bloom

We collaborate with organisations, employers, community groups, and purpose-driven leaders who believe women over 40 deserve more visibility, more support, and more opportunities to thrive.

Why Partner With Us

Create Meaningful Impact Together

Bloom partners with organisations that want to champion inclusion, create pathways for experienced women, and support career reinvention at every stage of life.

Whether you are looking to recruit talented women, sponsor a program, deliver a workshop, or collaborate on a community initiative, we would love to hear from you.

  • ✓ Connect with a highly engaged community of women aged 40+
  • ✓ Support inclusive hiring, mentoring, and leadership development
  • ✓ Build a partnership aligned with real social and professional impact

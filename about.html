Our Story

Bloom was born from a simple truth: women over 40 deserve a space that sees their experience as a strength. Not a limitation.

Our Mission

Empowering Women to Redefine What Is Possible

Bloom was created to challenge the idea that career growth has an expiration date. We believe women in their 40s, 50s, 60s, and beyond bring wisdom, resilience, and strength the world needs now more than ever.

Our mission is to offer the tools, community, and opportunities that support women through career transitions with clarity, confidence, and respect. Whether you’re returning to work, exploring a new direction, or redefining your professional identity, Bloom is here to walk that path with you.

We’re not a job board. We’re a movement—one that values the depth of a woman’s experience and helps turn it into meaningful, purposeful work.

Our Journey

From Vision to Movement

What started as a small circle of women supporting each other through career change has grown into a global platform reaching thousands. These are the moments that shaped our journey.

2018 — The Spark

Bloom began as a small group of 15 women meeting weekly to share their experiences and support each other through career transitions.

2019 — First Programs

We launched our first structured program, the Career Reinvention Intensive, supporting 120 women in its first year with meaningful results.

2020 — Going Digital

As the world shifted to remote work, we expanded online—reaching women across 12 countries through virtual programs and coaching.

2022 — Corporate Partnerships

Forward-thinking companies began partnering with Bloom to connect with experienced women ready for new challenges and leadership roles.

2024 — 10,000 Strong

We celebrated supporting our 10,000th woman, with a 94% satisfaction rate and a growing global community.

2026 — The Future

Today, Bloom continues to grow—expanding programs, deepening partnerships, and reinforcing our belief that age is an asset, not a barrier.
